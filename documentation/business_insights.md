# Business Insights

## Key Insights

- Grinder category contributed the highest share of total revenue, making it the major revenue-driving product category.
- Sales showed seasonal demand patterns, with stronger performance during selected months.
- Top dealers contributed a major portion of total revenue, showing dependency on key dealer accounts.
- Regional sales performance varied across branches, which helps identify stronger and weaker sales regions.
- Some product categories showed stable demand, while some categories required further business attention.

## Business Recommendations

- Focus promotional campaigns during high-demand months to improve sales conversion.
- Maintain proper inventory planning for high-contribution categories.
- Strengthen relationships with top-performing dealers to protect major revenue contribution.
- Review low-performing product categories and identify reasons for weak movement.
- Use branch-wise performance dashboards for sales target planning and monitoring.
