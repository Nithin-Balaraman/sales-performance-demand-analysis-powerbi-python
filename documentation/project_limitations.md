# Project Limitations

This file explains the limitations of the Sales Performance and Demand Analysis project.

## Limitations

* The analysis is based on historical sales data from FY 2019-2020 and FY 2020-2021.
* The original company dataset is not uploaded due to confidentiality.
* Customer-level behavioral data was not available for deeper customer segmentation.
* Competitor pricing data was not available for price comparison analysis.
* Forecasting was not included because the project mainly focused on descriptive and statistical analysis of available sales data.
* External market factors such as economic conditions, festival demand, and competitor campaigns were not included in the dataset.

## Why These Limitations Matter

These limitations show that the project findings are based only on the available internal sales data. Even with these limitations, the project provides useful insights about product performance, dealer contribution, regional sales, and demand patterns.
