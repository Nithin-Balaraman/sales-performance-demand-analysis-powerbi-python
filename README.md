# Sales Performance and Demand Analysis using Power BI and Python

## Project Overview

This project analyzes historical sales transaction data to understand sales performance, product contribution, regional performance, dealer contribution, and demand patterns.

The project uses Power BI for dashboard creation and Python for statistical analysis. The main purpose is to convert raw sales data into meaningful business insights that can support sales planning, product strategy, dealer management, and demand analysis.

## Dataset Overview

* Dataset Type: Secondary sales transaction data
* Records Analyzed: 4.5 lakh+ sales records
* Time Period: FY 2019-2020 and FY 2020-2021
* Final Columns Used: 27
* Main Focus Areas: Sales performance, product category analysis, regional sales, dealer contribution, and demand classification

## Key Business Insights

* Grinder category contributed the highest share of total revenue.
* Sales showed seasonal demand patterns across months.
* Top dealers contributed a major portion of total revenue.
* Regional sales performance varied across branches.
* Some product categories showed stable demand, while some required further business attention.

## Statistical Analysis Summary

Python was used to validate dashboard findings through statistical techniques.

* Correlation analysis showed a very weak positive relationship between quantity sold and sales value.
* One-way ANOVA showed significant differences in sales value across product categories.
* Linear regression showed that quantity sold alone does not strongly explain sales value.
* Independent sample t-test showed a significant difference in average transaction revenue between FY 2019-2020 and FY 2020-2021.
* Coefficient of variation helped compare sales stability across product categories.

## Business Recommendations

* Focus promotional campaigns during high-demand months.
* Maintain better inventory planning for high-contribution categories.
* Strengthen relationships with top-performing dealers.
* Review low-performing product categories and identify reasons for weak movement.
* Use branch-wise dashboards for sales target planning and monitoring.
